Get life’s admin off your mind

Digvy holds the infrequent life events that slip through the cracks so you don’t have to remember them: recurring maintenance, subscriptions, renewals, appointments, and the free-trial cancel-by deadlines that quietly turn into charges. It surfaces what needs action now and keeps a searchable history of everything else.

What Digvy keeps track of

  • Recurring maintenance — HVAC filters, gutter cleaning, oil changes, and other every-few-months chores.
  • Subscriptions & renewals — domains, memberships, and insurance policies that come due once a year.
  • Appointments — the dentist, eye exam, or annual physical that’s easy to let slide.
  • Cancel-by deadlines — free trials and auto-renewals you need to cancel before a specific date.
  • One-time reference records — warranty details, model numbers, and account numbers you just need to find later.

How it works

  1. Add or import your items. Type them in, pick from starter suggestions, or import a CSV.
  2. See what needs action. The dashboard surfaces what’s overdue, due soon, or approaching a cancel-by deadline.
  3. Find everything else instantly. Everything you’re tracking stays in a searchable history, out of the way until you need it.

Frequently asked questions

How is Digvy different from a to-do app or calendar?

To-do apps and calendars are built for things happening today or this week. Digvy is built for events that recur on long, irregular cycles — every 3 months, every year, or on a one-off deadline. It surfaces only what needs action now and keeps everything else searchable.

Can Digvy remind me before a free trial renews?

Yes. Digvy tracks cancel-by deadlines as a first-class field, flags them as they approach, and includes them in a daily email digest so you can cancel in time instead of getting charged.

How much does Digvy cost?

Digvy is free to use. Create an account with an email and password to get started.
